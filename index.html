Dear Mansi, Today is Propose Day

Not the kind that demands clarity.
But the kind that simply speaks.

I know we don’t stand on the same page.
And that’s okay — I truly mean that.

But somehow,
you still hold a space in my life
that feels calm, familiar, and quietly important.

Not everything needs a definition

Some bonds aren’t meant to be explained.
They just exist —
in conversations that linger,
in comfort that arrives without asking.

Somewhere between black and white,
there are softer shades where moments breathe.

I’m not asking you for more.
I’m not asking you to change anything.

I only wanted you to know this —

Your presence makes my days lighter,
my thoughts quieter,
and some silences feel less empty.

♡

So this is my proposal —

To honesty without expectation.
To connection without pressure.
To letting this space remain gentle,
exactly as it is.

— from someone who values you, deeply and quietly
